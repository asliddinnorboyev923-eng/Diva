Create a modern, minimalistic luxury website for a professional furniture and carpet cleaning service company.

STYLE & DESIGN:
- Minimalist, premium, luxury aesthetic
- Clean layout with lots of white space
- Soft neutral color palette (white, beige, light gray, with dark accents)
- Smooth micro-interactions and subtle animations (fade-in, hover effects, soft transitions)
- Use high-quality icons and visuals
- Focus on trust, cleanliness, and professionalism

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Elegant sans-serif font (modern and readable)
- Clear hierarchy (large headings, clean body text)

LANGUAGE:
- The website must support two languages: Uzbek and English
- Include a language switcher in the navbar (UZ / EN)

LAYOUT STRUCTURE:

1. HERO SECTION:
- Strong headline: emphasize deep cleaning, hygiene, and premium service
- Subheadline explaining benefits (removing stains, bacteria, freshness)
- CTA button: "Order Cleaning" / "Buyurtma berish"
- Background: clean home interior or cleaning process visual

2. SERVICES SECTION:
- Furniture cleaning
- Carpet cleaning
- Mattress cleaning
- Each service with icon + short description
- Cards with hover animation

3. PROCESS (IMPORTANT FOCUS):
- Step-by-step cleaning process (very important for trust)
- Example:
  1. Inspection
  2. Vacuuming
  3. Deep cleaning
  4. Drying
- Use icons and smooth animations
- Visually engaging timeline or steps UI

4. BEFORE / AFTER SECTION:
- Visual comparison (slider or side-by-side)
- Show real cleaning results

5. WHY CHOOSE US:
- Key benefits:
  - Eco-friendly products
  - Fast service
  - Experienced team
  - Affordable premium quality
- Use icons and minimal cards

6. TESTIMONIALS:
- Customer reviews
- Clean card layout with subtle animation

7. CTA SECTION:
- Strong selling block
- "Book your cleaning today"
- Button with standout design

8. CONTACT SECTION:
- Phone number
- Telegram / WhatsApp
- Location
- Simple contact form

NAVBAR:
- Clean sticky navbar
- Logo on left
- Menu items on right
- Language switcher (UZ | EN)
- CTA button

FOOTER:
- Minimal
- Contacts + social links

ANIMATIONS:
- Smooth and fast (not heavy)
- Micro-interactions on buttons and cards
- Scroll-based fade-ins

GOAL:
- The website should feel premium, trustworthy, and conversion-focused
- Designed to turn visitors into customers
